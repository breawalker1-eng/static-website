Favorite Musicians

One of my favorite singers is Adele. She creates soulful pop ballads and has had a greatly
successful career with multiple awards including two diamond albums. She is one of my favorites
because I think she has a beautiful voice and her poetic songwriting is amazing. I also like how
unique she is as an artist.

Radiohead is my favorite band. They are a grammy award winning alternative rock band. They are my
favorite group makes songs that make you sit down and think about the deeper meaning of it. I also
like that they are very philanthropic and have spread awareness for multiple causes.

My all time favorite artist is Whitney Houston. Her music is in the category of both R&B and Pop and She
is greatly considered to be one of the best singers of all time by numerous music critics.
She is my favorite musician because of how diverse she is with not only her songs, but also her voice.
